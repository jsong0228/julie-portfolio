Brand Identity + Website

CARE PROVIDE

Care Provide is an agency dedicated to helping families find the right care provider for their loved ones, particularly seniors. The company developed its own holistic care system, the INSPIRED™ Framework, to guide and train caregivers in delivering thoughtful, comprehensive personal care.

The logo mark visually represents this philosophy. The flower symbol embodies warmth, compassion, and growth, while each of the eight petals represents one component of the INSPIRED™ Framework — reflecting the structured yet nurturing approach at the heart of the organization.

Landing page design focused on creating a clear and approachable user experience that communicates trust, comfort, and professionalism. Soft color palettes, spacious layouts, and clean typography reinforce the brand’s compassionate, human-centered mission. The layout was structured to guide users through information in a calm and intuitive way while maintaining clarity and accessibility.
