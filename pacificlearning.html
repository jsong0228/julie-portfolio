Brand Identity + Learning Platform

PACIFIC LEARNING

Pacific Learning is an educational company focused on K–5 dual-language learning systems. The logo combines an open book with ocean waves, reflecting the company’s name while symbolizing education, knowledge, and learning. It can also be interpreted as a learning window—opening up new opportunities and perspectives through education.

A vibrant and approachable color palette was developed to create a cohesive brand experience across digital and print applications while supporting engaging and dynamic content.

Pacific Learning is a K–5 dual-language learning platform designed to make language acquisition engaging and structured. Built around themed units, each learning experience guides students through vocabulary, listening, reading, and quiz-based activities to reinforce understanding from multiple perspectives. The interface emphasizes clarity, progression, and motivation through visual progress indicators and a simple star-based reward system.

The dashboard is designed to support consistent learning habits. The Today’s Progress section helps students track their daily activity and revisit their progress throughout the day, encouraging them to complete lessons and stay engaged. The Achievements section highlights earned badges, allowing students to visualize their accomplishments and stay motivated over time.
